SAIC | ATS 3013 | More-than-human Sonic Ecologies: DIY Hardware for Site-specific Performance and Installation

Group Presentations

Presentation Criteria

Overall

You and 2-3 of your classmates will prepare a 20-30 minute presentation on an area of Sound, Performance, and Installation. Your presentation should cover the items below.

Artists
  • For each artist, present 1 or 2 artworks
  • Discuss how the work relates to the topic / technique
  • Read and discuss the artist's descriptions of these works (if available, if not, maybe don't pick that artwork!)
  • Give your own sense of the work: what do you see, how does it make you feel, what do you think it's about?
Your own connections

How do your interests intersect with this topic / technique? How might you continue to pursue this at this school or in your future work?
