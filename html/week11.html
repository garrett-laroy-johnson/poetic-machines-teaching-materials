Project 1

Using p5*js, use the canvas to create an interactive artwork. It may be a short comic or animation, cubistic and abstract. It my be inspired by some of the works we've seen before, or completely your own.

Required Elements

The following elements *must* be in your piece

  • if/else to structure interaction
  • mouseX, mouseY to drive interaction
  • At least (2) *types* of 2D primitives
  • A for loop
Tips

Start by getting all your elements to work inside a single sketch. Use print() or console.log() to test that things are working.

It doesn't need to be a masterpiece -- think of it as a study.

Consider how you use the interactivity. The example of interactivity from the page was pretty simple. How can the interactivity help to drive the narrative or drama of your piece?

We've used for loops to draw rows and columns. You could do that, or you could break out of the grid. The random()function may be your friend here.
